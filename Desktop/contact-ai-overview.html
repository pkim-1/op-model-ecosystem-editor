The Problem

🔀

Fragmented Data

Contact info scattered across Partner Central, Salesforce, TravelAds, and marketing tools with no unified view.

❌

Wrong / Unreachable Contacts

Invalid emails, retired contacts, and generic inboxes that never get a response.

🎯

Wrong Persona

Outreach lands on IT instead of revenue or marketing decision-makers who can act.

🔇

No Conversation Continuity

Objections, commitments, and context buried in notes across disconnected systems.

Result: Missed revenue, wasted outreach, and inconsistent partner experience

What is Contact AI?

A partner contact intelligence layer that creates a unified, AI-enriched view of the humans behind lodging partners.

Powers smarter coaching, sales, and marketing by turning fragmented contact data into actionable intelligence.

Five Components

🏗️

Unified Contact Data Foundation

Consolidates contacts across all systems. ML-powered deduplication creates a single Contact 360 record.

✅

Reachability & Enrichment

Activity and reachability scores with validated contact info. Know who is actually reachable.

⏰

Channel & Timing Preferences

Historical responsiveness patterns. Predicts best time and channel for each contact.

👤

Persona & Role Intelligence

Maps decision rights across the organization. Infers persona, seniority, and influence.

💬

Intent & Sentiment Layer

LLM analysis of transcripts and communications. Extracts objections, commitments, and sentiment signals.

Key Use Cases

📡

Reachability

Ensure outreach reaches valid, active humans -- not dead emails or generic inboxes.

📊

Channel Optimization

Pick the best channel and timing for each contact based on historical engagement.

🧠

Persona-Aware Coaching

Adapt recommendations and talking points to the contact's role and decision authority.

🤝

Sentiment Continuity

Share a common view of partner sentiment and history across all teams.
